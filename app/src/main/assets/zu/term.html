Izinqubo Zokusetshenziswa

1. Umboniso

Tholukisa kwi-WhatsApp Media Recovery App. Ngokulanda futhi ukusetshenziswa le app, uqinisekisa ukuthi uthandaze ukwethula izinqubo zokusetshenziswa. Lezi zinqubo ziyatshala ukusetshenziswa kwakho kwe-app futhi konke okuyithengisayo ngokusetshenziswa kwalo.

2. Ukusetshenziswa kwe-App

Uyakwazi ukusetshenzisa i-WhatsApp Media Recovery App kuphela ukuze uthole izifayela zemedia kusukela kwi-aplikeshini yakho ye-WhatsApp. Awukwazi ukusetshenzisa le app ngokwesilungu, ngaphandle kwezidingo, noma ngaphandle kwemishwana ethembekile.

3. Ukukhanya Komthetho

Ukukhanya kwakho kungenzeka. Sisebenzisa futhi sizilondolozele ezinye izinqubo njengoba kuvame ukusasazwa kwethu Kwesiko Lokukhanya. Ngesetshenziswa le app, uyavuma ukuqondisa kanye nokusetshenziswa kwemiphumela yakho njengoba kuvumelekileyo kwi-Kwesiko Lokukhanya.

4. Umqulu Wezithuthi

I-WhatsApp Media Recovery App kanye nabezinhlelo zayo zonke, okuyinto kufanele ibe nemibhalo, izifanekiso, izithombe, iziqondiso, kanye nezifundo ezinjalo, zingase zibe nesiqinisekiso sokuphatha. Ungakhohlwa ukwenza, ukwakhiwa, ukuguqula, noma ukwakha izinto zedala ngesivivinyo esifanayo.

5. Inkathi Yokusondela Uthando

I-WhatsApp Media Recovery App isetshenziselwa "njalo" ngaphandle kwemibhalo noma izivumelwano. Asivumeli ukusondela okubalulekile, ukuncikeleka, noma ukuphathwa kwe-app noma izinsiza zakhe. Usetshenzisa le app ngokuphila kwakho.

6. Isicelo Sokugcina

Eminyakeni engaphezu kwezinye, abaphathi besebeqala ukwengeza noma ukwenza uphayo olweqile. Kuyodinga kube nezibulawa noma ezibulungisa izinqubo zokusetshenziswa ukwenza esikhathini esizayo, hhayi ngenxa yezimo ezenzakalayo.

7. Izigaba

Siqinisekisa ukuthi sihlinzeke ukwengeza noma ukunciphisa izinqubo zokusetshenziswa ngokushesha noma ngezikhathi. Kuyiqiniso lakho ukuvakashela izigaba ngokwezigaba. Ngokwenza okunye ngokwesikhathi, uzikhululekile ukwenza upayivi oluphelele.

8. Umbhali Wengxenye

Lezi zinqubo zokusetshenziswa zizosungulwa futhi zitholakale kanye nokuthembekile kwezinkolo zika [Umbhali wakho]. Yonke imikhankaso esekupheleni yokukhathazeka kusukela kwi-app noma lezi zinqubo kuyoba phansi kwesigaba esiphakamayo.

9. Thinta Nathi

Uma unezinkinga noma ushintshe ngezi zinqubo zokusetshenziswa, sicela uthinte nathi ku- apps@dzinemedia.com
